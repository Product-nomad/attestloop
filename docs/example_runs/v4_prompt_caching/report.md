# Attestation report — EU Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
**Source:** [112367](https://ec.europa.eu/newsroom/dae/redirection/document/112367)
**Run:** `20260430-112932`

## Executive summary
Attestloop assessed "112367" against EU Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation 2024/1689) and identified 69 binding obligations. Of these, 45 were mapped to one or more subcategories of NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0; 24 obligations had no high-confidence mapping. The full obligation list and proposed control mapping follow; this assessment is not legal advice.
