## Executive summary
Attestloop assessed "112367" against EU Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation 2024/1689) and identified 18 binding obligations. Of these, 18 were mapped to one or more subcategories of NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0; 0 obligations had no high-confidence mapping. The full obligation list and proposed control mapping follow; this assessment is not legal advice.
